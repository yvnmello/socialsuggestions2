Purpose:
The purpose of this application is to provide quick and accessible social activity ideas tailored to separate times of the day. By offering a wide variety of suggestions that suit morning, afternoon, evening, and even late-night hours, the app aims to help users effortlessly organize and progress their social lives. This application makes it simple to discover activities that fit both their schedule and interests, making social planning more enjoyable and stress-free.
